Services

We specialize in pool renovation and comprehensive deep cleaning. Below are examples of our work and typical offerings — click to request a quote.

Renovation

Complete pool renovation including surface restoration, tile and coping replacement, structural repairs, pump and equipment upgrades, and waterproofing. We manage projects from inspection to handover with clear timelines and quality guarantees.

  • Surface resurfacing (plaster, quartz, pebble)
  • Tile & coping replacement
  • Equipment upgrades (pumps, filters, heaters)
  • Leak detection and structural repairs

Typical timeline: 1–4 weeks depending on scope.

Deep Cleaning

Thorough cleaning service focused on algae removal, stain treatment, filter service, and chemical balancing. Ideal for seasonal cleanups, post-storm recovery, or preparing pools for reopening.

  • Algae & biofilm removal
  • Filter cleaning and inspection
  • Chemical balance and water testing
  • Stain treatment and vacuuming

Typical visit: 1–2 days depending on condition.
